Featured Projects

Custom Home: The Bentley Residence

The Bentley Residence is a testament to our commitment to creating stunning, one-of-a-kind custom homes. Designed in close collaboration with the Bentley family, this project began with a vision of a timeless, elegant home that balances functionality with luxurious details. Using the finest materials and expert craftsmanship, we brought their dream to life. The exterior features hand-selected stonework paired with custom wood accents, creating a warm yet sophisticated facade. High-quality soffits ensure proper ventilation while seamlessly blending with the home’s architectural elements.

Luxury Renovation: The Windsor Estate

The Windsor Estate renovation showcases our dedication to transforming homes into luxurious, functional spaces. This project focused on revitalizing a dated property while enhancing its charm and sophistication. The exterior received a fresh look with premium wood soffits and custom stonework, adding warmth and timeless elegance. Updated windows and doors not only improved energy efficiency but also elevated the home's curb appeal.

Commercial Project: Marda Loop Apartments

The Marda Loop Apartments highlight our ability to deliver modern, efficient commercial projects that cater to urban living. Located in Calgary’s vibrant Marda Loop district, this project involved designing and constructing a contemporary apartment complex that balances style, durability, and functionality. The exterior features clean lines and a sleek facade, enhanced with high-quality finishes, including durable cladding and expertly installed soffits for a modern aesthetic. Large windows and spacious balconies bring in natural light while creating a welcoming, open feel for residents.
